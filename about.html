About Me

Char Jones (Charlotte Louise Jones) is an actor based in NYC with ties to LA and Chicago. She made her professional debut at the age of 8 as Bo Grodon in Off The Map and has continued to pursue acting ever since.

Charlotte holds both a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science from Northwestern University, where she also studied Theatre and Dance. Her diverse acting career spans Film, Television, regional theater, Off-Broadway, and most recently, a performance at La MaMa in NYC.

She is classically trained and has studied with the Royal Shakespeare Company, Juilliard Extension Program, T. Schreiber Studio, Katie Cappiello, UCB, and Prague Shakespeare Company. In addition to acting, Charlotte is a trained aerialist, dancer, and diver.

Outside of performance, Charlotte loves Shakespeare, her family, playing Catan, Citibiking around NYC, and exploring new creative adventures.
